HELLO GUYS! I think you should all play Rust, it is a very nice and welcoming game! When you spawn, you wake up in a beach with only a rock! Then you have to break the trees and rocks to give you resources so you can advance. But thats not all! When you spawn there's also these guys with AK's and Semi-automatics shooting you and calling you names as you arrive on the beach, kinda like D-Day! Its really a cool experience for those who like history.
